'Fuzzy' the Peach

Fuzzy comes from the beautiful and cold orchards in China. Travelling far and wide, Fuzzy is a true global citizen. Proud of keeping it 'au naturale', he would never get rid of his peach fuzz. But it can make him sweaty, especially during this long and crowded trans atlantic flights. Fuzzy has sensitive skin and prefers materails that protect it. He likes a moderate climate and avoids extremes on either end.

Fuzzy's Needs

  • to Find pants that are breathable. Fuzzy can get sweaty.
  • prefers softer materials, Fuzzy bruises like a peach!
  • Likes to stay away from the sun but not in too much cold either.

'Shiny' the Nectarine

Shiny is a proper californian diva! Big in the LA fashion scene and an advocate of the organic movement, she prefers natural materials that are soft on her skin and her environment. Although raised in Cali, She's a true Georgian peach, sweet and kind at heart. Some even call her juicy but she just smiles and pretends to be naive. She got rid of her peach fuzz because She feels she's a proper lady.

Shiny's Needs

  • Prefers natural materials like paper or cotton but will tolerate sythetic in a short run.
  • Needs something that can keep the moisure away. Skincare is of utmost importance.
  • Thrives in colder environment and needs something to stay cozy

'Plop' the Flat Peach

A true hispanic beauty, Plop loves the mediteranean life. But, that doesn't stop Plop from flying all over the world. Some also call him donut, but he does not like such physical remarks. He embraces this bodacious curves and looks for clothing that complements them, while keeping him comfortable in the travel. He needs materials that are soft to keep him comfortable in the warm, coastal climate

Plop's Goals

  • A little body-conscious, pefects something that cemplements those curves
  • Likes late spring and summer so prefers materials that beat the heat
  • Is unique, and would prefer the same
